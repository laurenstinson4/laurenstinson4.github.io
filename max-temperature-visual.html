Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (i.e. as the latitude approaches 0 degrees). As we know, the seasons in the southern hemisphere are opposite of those in the northern hemisphere, so it's interesting to see the temperatures so different at the same time of year.
